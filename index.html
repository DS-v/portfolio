About

I'm a product manager working at the intersection of applied AI and B2B platforms. As the first PM at NiryatAI (formerly WorldRef), I've owned the 0→1 build of a full-stack cross-border procurement platform — turning a bespoke, trader-led business into a standardized workflow spanning RFQ through order execution.

Before product, I was a data scientist, which shapes how I treat models: as components, not solutions. The leverage is almost always in the data formation, the evaluation, and the risk boundaries — not the model itself.
